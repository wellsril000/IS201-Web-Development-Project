  1. How to Make a Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwich
    • Get out two peices of bread, jelly, peanut butter, knife
    • Spread jelly on one side of one peice of bread with the knife
    • Wipe off the knife, and spread peanut butter on one side of the other peice of bread
    • Put the peices of bread together with the jelly and peanut butter sides touching
    • Enjoy!
